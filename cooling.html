Cooling

PC Cooling: Description and Function

  • PC cooling helps keep the computer's components, like the CPU and GPU, from overheating. It uses fans, heat sinks, and liquid cooling to move heat away from these parts, ensuring they run efficiently and don't get damaged.

Cooling Parts and Troubleshooting Tips

1. Fans

  • Function: Move air to cool components.
  • Troubleshooting:
    • Not spinning: Check if they're plugged in or if there's dust blocking them.
    • Making noise: Clean or replace if damaged.

2. Heat Sinks

  • Function: Absorb and spread heat away from the CPU or GPU.
  • Troubleshooting:
    • Not seated properly: Reattach securely.
    • Too hot: Ensure thermal paste is applied properly and not worn out.

3. Thermal Paste

  • Function: Helps transfer heat from the CPU to the heat sink.
  • Troubleshooting:
    • Old or dried out: Reapply new thermal paste if temperatures rise too much.
